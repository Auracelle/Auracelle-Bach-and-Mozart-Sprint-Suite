# Auracelle Bach - Cognitive Amplifier for Governance Research

**An advanced cognitive enhancement tool for AI governance policy research and analysis**

## Overview

Auracelle Bach Cognitive Amplifier is a specialized research tool designed to amplify the cognitive capabilities of researchers, academics, and policy analysts working on complex AI governance challenges. This system enhances analytical capacity through structured frameworks, mathematical modeling, and interactive simulation environments.

## Key Features

- **Cognitive Enhancement Tools**: Structured frameworks to amplify research thinking
- **Mathematical Modeling**: Bayesian networks, game theory, and optimization models
- **Interactive Simulations**: Real-time policy scenario exploration
- **Research Amplification**: Tools designed to enhance academic productivity
- **Evidence Synthesis**: Systematic integration of research findings
- **Policy Stress Testing**: Simulate policy outcomes under various conditions

## Target Audience

- PhD candidates and doctoral researchers
- Academic research teams
- Policy research institutions
- Think tanks and research centers
- Graduate-level coursework in AI governance
- Interdisciplinary research collaborations

## About E-AGPO-HT Framework

Powered by the proprietary E-AGPO-HT (Evans Accelerated Governance Policy Optimization - Hierarchical Theory):

- **Stratum III**: g-GWC (General Governance Wargaming Capacity)
- **Stratum II**: 7 Broad Governance Capabilities + 4 AI acceleration factors
- **Stratum I**: ~40 Narrow Operational Factors

The Cognitive Amplifier focuses specifically on enhancing research capacity within this framework.
